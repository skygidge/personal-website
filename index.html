Case studyTRANSCRIPT → SCORECARD

Sales Analysis: David

David was mic'd on a shoot because I was recording him. After transcribing all the footage, Claude could turn what he did and said on the floor into a data-driven snapshot of his performance — his actual sales count came out correct. The social read still needs a human, but David recognized the analysis as accurate and committed to improving. Not far off the wearable assistant Apple is rumored to be building.
